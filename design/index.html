A-Frame 3D Scene

In my Introduction to Computational Media class, I used HTML and A-Frame to make this 3D scene of a cherry blossom tree. Follow this link to navigate the 3D scene. Since I was new to A-Frame, I went for a simplistic look. The tree sits on a cylinder of grass. Its trunk is made of cones that taper slightly instead of coming to a point. Clusters of flowers are represented by dodecahedrons of various sizes, and individual flower petals in the form of small cylindrical disks float around the tree. I thought these floating petals would be a good way to take advantage of the three-dimensional medium.
