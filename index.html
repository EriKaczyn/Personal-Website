Erika Kaczynski

Hello! My name is Erika, and I am a senior at the Georgia Institute of Technology majoring in Computational Media, a degree program that combines computer science and design. My concentrations are Media and Games. I hope to one day work as a game developer. On this website, you can find projects that I have worked on and links to my LinkedIn, GitHub, and resume.

For the past year, I have been working at Lockheed Martin as a software engineering intern, modeling graphical components, unit testing, and debugging software for the C-5 aircraft. This has been a great opportunity to work with a team of other software engineers, as well as systems engineers, to contribute to the project and improve my skills. I'm excited to see what I accomplish after graduation!

I first started coding in high school when I took AP Computer Science A. It came naturally to me, and I eventually found myself competing in a coding competition and co-founding my school's Girls Who Code Club. I love exercising my creativity and creating projects that people can enjoy and that will persist long after I'm gone. Since I love making my ideas a reality, I also like digital and traditional art and creative writing. I hope you enjoy my website!
